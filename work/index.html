MeFree

Swift, Sketch, InVision

MeFree is an iOS social network app that helps people host and join impromptu hangouts with friends.

Learn about my experience working in an agile sprint group as the sole developer and designer, how I used user research to guide the design process, and how we collaborated together in order to launch our app to the Apple App Store in less than 4 months.

OK2TALK

Sketch, Marvel, Box

OK2TALK improves how the National Alliance on Mental Health (NAMI) gathers needed feedback from teenage participants who attend their mental health support-groups.

Learn how I collaborated with 14 talented designers at IBM Maelstrom to create OK2TALK. In this case study, I learned how to apply IBM Design Thinking to bring empathy to design, learn from competition in the market, and bring scalability to the heart of the process.

Check It Out

Stock Up

Sketch, Google Suite

Stock Up is a startup concept for bar managers that greatly improves their alcohol selection and ordering process by providing vendor information, a one-stop-shop for alcoholic orders, and analytics to manage and spice up their weekly selections.

Learn about my experience at 3 Day Startup, a weekend workshop where I worked with a starters to identify a market need, research and validate it, and finally design a brand and service to help meet it.
